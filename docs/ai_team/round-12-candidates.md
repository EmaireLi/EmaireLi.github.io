# Round 12 Candidate and Stop Set

Both evaluators score the same set. Unlike implementation rounds, C0 is an explicit stop decision. Select a feature only if it materially exceeds C0 on evidence-backed net value.

## C0. Stop the Improvement Loop

- **Outcome:** Preserve the existing static `Back to archive` ending and ship no Round 12 product change.
- **Evidence:** Core homepage, archive, mobile, project, search, and contact gaps have been addressed; current article ending already provides a resilient task-completing route.
- **Cost:** None beyond documentation and final verification.
- **Reopen triggers:** At least 12 posts, genuine series metadata, reader/analytics evidence of end-of-article drop-off, or consolidation to one canonical article generator.
- **Risk:** Leaves direct-landings with utility copy rather than a warmer editorial handoff.

## C1. Quiet Static Archive Signature

- **Value:** Explain that the article belongs to Alex's personal archive and offer First Reads plus all articles.
- **Surface:** Five current posts, checked-in template, editor renderer, XHS importer, styles, and parity checker—nine production ownership points.
- **Cost:** S–M.
- **Risk:** Existing header/footer already establish ownership and archive escape; repetition may feel promotional after intimate writing.

## C2. One Runtime Chronological Continuation

- **Value:** Offer exactly one clearly earlier/newer post at the article end.
- **Surface:** Shared runtime, manifest contract, current shells/template/generators or a mount strategy, styles, and fixtures for at least nine edge classes.
- **Cost:** M.
- **Risk:** Five heterogeneous posts limit reader value; encoding, edge direction, matching, fetch failure, and no-JS fallback broaden regression surface.

## C3. Static Generated Chronological Continuation

- **Value:** Provide the same one-link path without runtime dependency.
- **Surface:** Generator must rewrite article neighbors whenever publication order changes, plus all ownership/parity checks.
- **Cost:** M–L.
- **Risk:** Publication coupling and noisy multi-file rewrites are disproportionate at current corpus size.

## C4. Topic-Based Continuation

- **Value:** Continue within an exact tag when possible.
- **Surface:** Manifest/runtime plus transparent reason labels and broad/source-tag exclusions.
- **Cost:** M.
- **Risk:** Five posts, singleton tags, broad `生活`, and source label `小红书` cannot sustain a credible relevance promise.

## Hard exclusions

No embeddings, RAG, chatbot, personalization, carousel, or GPU visualization; none has an evaluation corpus or stronger visitor case.
