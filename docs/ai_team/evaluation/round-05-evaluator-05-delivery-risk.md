| Rank | Feature Family | Score | Delivery-Risk Read |
|---:|---|---:|---|
| 1 | Site Pipeline Lens | 8.5 | Best value/risk fit. Makes the website itself feel engineered: editor, import, manifest, archive, search, guestbook, deploy. Static HTML/CSS can carry most of it. |
| 2 | Curated Archive Lanes/Windows | 7.7 | Strong site-level discovery gain. Risk is metadata unevenness and stale manual picks, but a small curated version is worth it. |
| 3 | AI Boundary Ledger | 7.0 | Good trust signal if restrained. Shows responsible AI-assisted workflow without pretending the site is AI-powered. |
| 4 | Decision Receipts Strip | 6.9 | Useful proof of judgment and QA. Risk is that internal docs may feel too process-heavy for general visitors. |
| 5 | Reading Route Cards / Featured Reading Path | 6.9 | Low-cost discovery improvement. Good long-term homepage utility, but overlaps with existing Start Here routes unless it recommends concrete posts. |
| 6 | Contact Intent Panel | 6.8 | High conversion value and low implementation risk. Lower technical showcase, but practical. |
| 7 | Personal Positioning Microcopy Pass | 6.7 | Cheap and useful for hierarchy, but not enough as the main Round 05 feature. |
| 8 | Search Match Reasons/Context | 6.6 | Real product polish and decent technical signal, but it touches shared `script.js`. |
| 9 | Project Constraint Cards | 6.3 | Shows engineering maturity, but risks drifting back toward external project evaluation. |
| 10 | Account Identity Map | 6.2 | Low-risk cleanup. Helps personal coherence, but standalone value and technical showcase are modest. |
| 11 | Homepage Review Minimap | 5.9 | Could help navigation, but sticky/active nav adds mobile and accessibility risk. |
| 12 | Visual Archive Preview Strip | 5.7 | Adds warmth and visual texture, but image cropping, performance, and homepage distraction risk are nontrivial. |
| 13 | Evidence Freshness Markers | 5.5 | Trust upside is real, but stale freshness labels harm credibility. |
| 14 | Role Readiness Scorecard | 5.1 | Recruiter-readable, but self-scoring can feel inflated and resume-like. |

## Recommended Features

1. Primary: Site Pipeline Lens
2. Secondary: Curated Archive Lanes/Windows
3. Small bundle only: Contact Intent Panel + Personal Positioning Microcopy Pass

## Eliminated For Round 05

- Role Readiness Scorecard: too self-evaluative.
- Evidence Freshness Markers: maintenance risk outweighs value unless automated.
- Visual Archive Preview Strip: useful later, but image/layout QA risk is higher than archive-lane value.
- Homepage Review Minimap: defer unless homepage length becomes a proven problem.
- Project Constraint Cards: partially outside the corrected scope because it centers project evaluation more than the website experience itself.
